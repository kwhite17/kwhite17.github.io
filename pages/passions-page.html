InterVarsity

InterVarsity is a Christian ministry that focuses on developing students and faculty in the Christian faith. My time in college with InterVarsity really helped guide me in the pursuit of growing my faith and being a servant of the Lord.

323 For the King

323 is a Christian community outreach organization based out of Highland Park, Michigan that provides a space for kids K-12 to hang out, eat pizza, and hear God's Word once a week. It's been an incredible experience hanging out with kids and watching them grow as they hear lessons from the Bible.
